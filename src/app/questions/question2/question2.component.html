Anaemia

Anemia is common in patients with heart failure (HF) and reduced ejection fraction (HFrEF) or preserved ejection fraction (HFpEF) and is associated with worse long-term outcomes. The anemia itself can worsen cardiac function, both because it causes cardiac stress through tachycardia and increased stroke volume, and because it can cause a reduced renal blood flow and fluid retention, adding further stress to the heart
