Age of the patient

Congestive heart failure affects people of all ages, from children and young adults to the middle-aged and the elderly. Almost 1.4 million persons with CHF are under 60 years of age. CHF is present in 2 percent of persons age 40 to 59.

There are a number of factors that may make heart failure more likely, despite your age. For that reason it is one of the variables that is included in this prediction system.
