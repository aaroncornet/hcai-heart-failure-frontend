Predicting Heart Failure

Cardiovascular diseases are not uncommon in our society anymore and cause a lot of yearly deaths. Heart failure is on of the most dangerous ones in the lineup of cardiovascular diseases and an early diagnosis could help in the fighting chance against such ailment.

This application is meant to be used by physicians who want to verify their well informed diagnosis or working theory and to verify if the patient should be referred to a medical professional specialized in the treatment of cardiovascular diseases.
